So, first thing first: NEVER water your plants on a schedule! Know your plants, check the soil, learn about their necessities, and act accordingly.

Many plants are extremely sensitive to overwatering, and many to drought.

The watering frequency reminder should be used to remind yourself that your plant exists, and to check whether it requires your attention or not.
